Live Wedding Painting

The artist arrives on site two hours before the event to paint in the background, followed by 4-5 hours of live painting of the couples chosen scene. The painting typically includes the guests of honour, the venue and party details. Your guests can watch the painting unfolds before their eyes.

Entertainment

Most couples prefer to have their ceremony painted, the first dance, or the “I DO” moment. Whatever scene is chosen, the live process will entertain guests and create a memorable experiences for you and your guests.

Traditional Heirloom

Before the invention of modern photography, paintings documented life. Live painting is a traditional plein air painting. It can still be the most personal way to document your experience

Commissions
