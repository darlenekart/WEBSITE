Live Wedding Painting

Creating a Memorable Experience

Your Wedding Scene

Most couples prefer to have their ceremony painted, the first dance, or the “I DO” moment. However, the scenery you want me to paint is up to you! Of course, we would discuss your options during the consultation. If you change your mind that is OK! I just need to know your final decision the week before the wedding.

Canvas Sizes

Each painting is completed on gallery wrapped 1.5” profile canvas using the highest quality artist grade paints. I use Holbein and Golden heavy acrylic paints. Available canvas sizes: 16”x20”, 18”x24”, 20”x24”, 24”x36”.

Live Painting

On the day of your wedding, I arrive 2 hours before your guests arrive. I bring the Canvas, Easel, Paints and Brushes on site 2 hours prior to paint in the scenery. I only require a water source to rinse my brushes and an available outlet for my easel lamp. I will set up in a space that will not cause an eye sore to the wedding.

As your guests come in, I will paint for the next 4-5 hours and paint in the couple, key details and guests. Your guests will be wow’d by the painting unfolding in front of them. Final touch ups will be completed in the studio, varnished and shipped/delivered to you. The finished painting is a keepsake that will last a lifetime.

How much should you expect to pay for a wedding painting? Packages start at CAD $1,400.00 (before tax) and will increase depending on the canvas size (starting at 16”x20”), collection type, and additional wedding details. Local travel is included. Travel costs are additional if +100 km from Regina, Saskatchewan. A 20% non-refundable retainer is required at the time of booking.
